# Snake-and-ladder-game-in-C
C program for creating &amp; simulating a Snake &amp; Ladder Game.

// Coded by: Rohit Chowdhury.  /\\('-')/\ 

//Typed and compiled with <TDM-GCC 4.9.2 64-bit profiling> compiler in Dev-C++ 5.11 . 

Constraints and Rules:-

1.The game will begin with 1 dice value.

2.Both the player location is shown in different colours.

3.Snakes:- 98 to  80, 94 to 12, 92 to 67, 56 to 48, 42 to 1, 61 to 43, 25 to 3.

4.Ladder:- 7 to 30, 16 to 33, 20 to 38, 36 to 83, 63 to 81, 86 to 97, 50 to 68, 71 to 89.

5.landing on a ladder lets you climb up, while landing on a snake sends you down.

Enjoy playing...
